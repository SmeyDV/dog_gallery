Borador

Borador is a little-known dog breed and not yet officially recognized as a real breed. It is recognized in the United States of America. Its name derives from the union of names of the two breeds that constitute it (Border Collie and Labrador Retriever) for simplification. The Borador is an outgoing mixed breed dog that’s a combination of the Labrador Retriever and the Border Collie. They’re becoming popular amongst active owners looking for an energetic and curious dog that combines the kindly natures of both breeds. The Borador can be a first cross (with one Labrador and one Collie parent) or they can be bred back to one of the original breeds or be two Boradors bred together - so there are varieties in size, shape, coat types, and temperaments.
